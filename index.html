Nutrient management is a process of managing the amount, source, timing and method of nutrient application while minimizing the loss of nutrients which could create environmental problems and it has a primary goal of optimizing the productivity of a Farm. It includes developing nutrient budgets that consists of knowing the amount of nutrients that is already present in the soil and determining the amount of nutrients which is needed by the crop accounting for all the potential sources of the nutrients.

How soil test beneficial for the farmers of India ?

Soil testing is a valuable tool for farmers in India, as it helps them to determine the nutrient content and fertility of their soil. This knowledge can then be used to make informed decisions about which fertilizers to use and how much to apply, which can ultimately lead to increased crop yields and reduced costs.One of the main benefits of soil testing is that it can help farmers to identify nutrient deficiencies in their soil.
