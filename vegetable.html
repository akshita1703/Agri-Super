Less pH soil suitable crops

Crops that are sensitive to pH levels in the soil may not thrive or may even fail to germinate and grow if the pH is not within their optimal range. Some crops, such as blueberries and potatoes, prefer acidic soils with a pH of around 5.0 to 5.5, while others, such as sweet potatoes and tomatoes, prefer neutral to slightly alkaline soils with a pH of around 6.0 to 7.0.

Crops that are suitable for acidic soils include

Blueberries

Blueberries are highly sensitive to pH levels, and they require acidic soils with a pH between 4.5 and 5.5. If the soil is too alkaline, the blueberries will not be able to absorb the necessary nutrients, and they will not grow or produce well.

Potatoes

Potatoes also prefer acidic soils, with a pH between 4.5 and 5.5. Alkaline soils can lead to nutrient deficiencies, poor growth, and poor yields.

raspberries

Like blueberries, raspberries require acidic soils with a pH between 4.5 and 5.5. They will not thrive in alkaline soils.

Strawberries

Strawberries also require acidic soils with a pH between 4.5 and 5.5. They will not thrive in alkaline soils.

Crops that are less suitable for acidic soils include

Sweet potatoes .

Sweet potatoes prefer neutral to slightly alkaline soils with a pH between 6.0 and 7.0. They will not thrive in acidic soils.

Tomatoes

Tomatoes prefer neutral to slightly alkaline soils with a pH between 6.0 and 7.0. They will not thrive in acidic soils.

peppers

Peppers prefer neutral to slightly alkaline soils with a pH between 6.0 and 7.0. They will not thrive in acidic soils

Eggplants

Eggplants also prefer neutral to slightly alkaline soils with a pH between 6.0 and 7.0. They will not thrive in acidic soils.

It's important to note that the ideal pH range for a crop can vary depending on the variety and the specific growing conditions. Additionally, many crops can tolerate a range of pH levels, but may not perform as well outside of their optimal range. Therefore, it's important to test the pH of the soil before planting and make adjustments as necessary.
